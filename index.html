The Dollhouse

Four rooms, four memories

Each room of the dollhouse holds a scattered jigsaw puzzle. Nikki must parkour and climb through the oversized space to find every piece, reconstruct the memory image, and earn a fragment of the key that will set her free.

Gameplay

How it plays

Nikki's Dollhouse is a navigation puzzle - the level design is the puzzle.

1

Wake up tiny

Nikki wakes up inside the dollhouse. The front door is visible but locked. The cat appears. ....

2

Explore and climb

Each room is a giant obstacle course. Nikki can walk, run, jump, grab ledges and climb. Puzzle pieces are hidden in spaces that require exploration to reach - on top of furniture, behind objects, inside tight spaces.

3

Find and carry pieces

Press E to pick up a puzzle piece and carry it. Bring it back to the puzzle board on the floor. Pieces snap into place when dropped into the correct slot.

4

Complete the picture

When all pieces are placed the memory image appears a photograph from Nikki's past. A key fragment materialises. The next room's door unlocks.

5

Escape

Four rooms, four key fragments, one assembled key. Nikki walks to the front door, presses E, and steps through. She wakes up.

Characters

Who's in the dollhouse

Main character

Nikki

A stressed college student, home during semester break with a fever. Humanoid, rigged, with idle, walk, run, climb and interact animations. The player is her.

Narrator NPC

The Cat

The family cat - a toy come to life in the dream. Follows Nikki through the dollhouse. Gives hints only when asked. Knows more than it lets on.

Enemy — all rooms

Wind-up Toy

Patrols the floor. Detects Nikki when she gets close and chases her. Returns to patrol if she escapes. Contact deducts health.
