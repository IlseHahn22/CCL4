Storyline

Nikki a young collage student comes back to her parent’s house for a semester break, feeling overwhelmed with the idea of growing up as she is about to finish her last year of college, the stress and responsibility of adulthood seem to occupy her mind constantly. To get her mind off of it, she decides to go down the memory lane and remember what it was like to be a kid again. When she discovers her childhood doll house, a wave of nostalgia hits her and all she can wish for is to be small again, when nothing else mattered, except playing with her beloved childhood toy. Feeling tired after a long journey home, she decides to take a nap. Once she awakens, her surroundings are are not the same, as she is clearly not in her bedroom, but nevertheless, there is something very familiar about the place she finds herself in. Eventually, the realization hits - her wish came true: She turned small, quite literally, and now has to wander around the rooms of her dollhouse. Help Nikki collect objects and memory fragments to bring her back to her real life.

System Design

How the game is structured

Scenes - Four rooms, four memories

The dollhouse has 4 scenes — each room acts as the main scene as the player's journey progresses.

  • Kitchen room (Ilse)
  • Playroom (Sofija)
  • Bedroom (Mahmut)
  • Art room (Aruzhan)

The player starts in the kitchen and progresses by completing the task in each room. Tasks include finding and collecting specific objects.

Characters

Nikki —> main character. Player will be exploring the rooms throught the dollhouse where the goal is to find the right objects that will help Nikki return

Cat —> narrator. Guides Nikki throughout each room, giving directions and clues when asked.

Wind-up toy —> enemy. Patrols all rooms. When Nikki enters their detection range they start following her.

Audio

Background melody - In rooms there will be a music box melody with some sounds of nature(birds, wind and trees)

Character sounds - jumping, walking, grabbing, climbing

Other characters - each character has sounds corresponding to their role. (enemy → evil sound and etc)

Gameplay

How it plays

Nikki's Dollhouse is a navigation puzzle - the level design is the puzzle.

1

Wake up tiny

Nikki wakes up inside the dollhouse. The front door is visible but locked. The cat appears. ....

2

Explore and climb

Each room is a giant obstacle course. Nikki can walk, run, jump, grab ledges and climb. Puzzle pieces are hidden in spaces that require exploration to reach - on top of furniture, behind objects, inside tight spaces.

3

Find and carry pieces

Press E to pick up a puzzle piece and carry it. Bring it back to the puzzle board on the floor. Pieces snap into place when dropped into the correct slot.

4

Complete the picture

When all pieces are placed the memory image appears a photograph from Nikki's past. A key fragment materialises. The next room's door unlocks.

5

Escape

Four rooms, four key fragments, one assembled key. Nikki walks to the front door, presses E, and steps through. She wakes up.

Characters

Who's in the dollhouse

Main character

Nikki

A stressed college student, home during semester break with a fever. Humanoid, rigged, with idle, walk, run, climb and interact animations. The player is her.

Narrator NPC

The Cat

The family cat - a toy come to life in the dream. Follows Nikki through the dollhouse. Gives hints only when asked. Knows more than it lets on.

Enemy — all rooms

Wind-up Toy

Patrols the floor. Detects Nikki when she gets close and chases her. Returns to patrol if she escapes. Contact deducts health.
