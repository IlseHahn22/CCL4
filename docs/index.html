Storyline

Nikki a young collage student comes back to her parent’s house for a semester break, feeling overwhelmed with the idea of growing up as she is about to finish her last year of college, the stress and responsibility of adulthood seem to occupy her mind constantly. To get her mind off of it, she decides to go down the memory lane and remember what it was like to be a kid again. When she discovers her childhood doll house, a wave of nostalgia hits her and all she can wish for is to be small again, when nothing else mattered, except playing with her beloved childhood toy.

Feeling tired after a long journey home, she decides to take a nap. Once she awakens, her surroundings are are not the same, as she is clearly not in her bedroom, but nevertheless, there is something very familiar about the place she finds herself in. Eventually, the realization hits - her wish came true: She turned small, quite literally, and now has to wander around the rooms of her dollhouse.

Help Nikki collect objects and memory fragments to bring her back to her real life.

System Design

How the game is structured

Scenes - Four rooms, four memories

The dollhouse has 4 scenes — each room acts as the main scene as the player's journey progresses.

  • Kitchen room (Ilse)
  • Playroom (Sofija)
  • Bedroom (Mahmut)
  • Art room (Aruzhan)

The player starts in the kitchen and progresses by completing the task in each room. Tasks include finding and collecting specific objects.

Characters

Nikki —> main character. Player will be exploring the rooms throught the dollhouse where the goal is to find the right objects that will help Nikki return

Cat —> narrator. Guides Nikki throughout each room, giving directions and clues when asked.

Wind-up toy —> enemy. Patrols all rooms. When Nikki enters their detection range they start following her.

Audio

Background melody - In rooms there will be a music box melody with some sounds of nature(birds, wind and trees)

Character sounds - jumping, walking, grabbing, climbing

Other characters - each character has sounds corresponding to their role. (enemy → evil sound and etc)

Player & Interaction

Movement -> WASD movement relative to camera direction with smooth rotation. Space to jump, F to climb ledges.

Interaction —> E key picks up and drops objects. Every interactable object implements a shared IInteractable interface. Puzzle pieces, small props, and doors are all interactable. Large furniture is physics-only and can be pushed by walking into it.

Puzzle system —> Each piece has a unique ID matched to a slot on the board. Carrying a piece to its correct slot and pressing E snaps it into place. Completing the board reveals a memory image and unlocks the next room.

System Infrastructure

Class relationships

Player

  • PlayerMovement —> WASD, camera-relative, Slerp rotation
  • PlayerJump —> BoxCast ground check, LayerMask, impulse force
  • PlayerClimb —> SphereCast ledge detection, smooth climb coroutine
  • PlayerInteract —> OverlapSphere, closest IInteractable, puzzle board detection

Interaction

  • IInteractable —> interface for all interactive objects
  • SmallProp —> pickup, carry arc, drop with physics
  • PuzzlePiece —> pickup, carry, snap to board slot, locked after placement
  • PushableObject —> physics-based push on collision, no pickup

Puzzle

  • PuzzleBoard —> ID matching, placement validation, completion trigger
  • PuzzleSlot —> slotID, snap point transform, fill state
  • On completion: memory image revealed, door to next room unlocked
  • GameManager tracks completion state across all four rooms
Gameplay

How it plays

Nikki's Dollhouse is a navigation puzzle - the level design is the puzzle.

1

Wake up tiny

Nikki wakes up inside the dollhouse. The front door is visible but locked. The cat appears.

2

Explore and climb

Each room is a giant obstacle course. Nikki can walk, run, jump, grab ledges and climb. Puzzle pieces are hidden in spaces that require exploration to reach - on top of furniture, behind objects, inside tight spaces.

3

Find and carry pieces

Press E to pick up a puzzle piece and carry it. Bring it back to the puzzle board on the floor. Pieces snap into place when dropped into the correct slot.

4

Complete the picture

After the fourth and final memory is restored, a key materialises. The cat nudges Nikki toward the front door.

5

Escape

Nikki walks to the front door, uses the key —> and wakes up.
