Storyline

Nikki a young collage student comes back to her parent’s house for a semester break, feeling overwhelmed with the idea of growing up as she is about to finish her last year of college, the stress and responsibility of adulthood seem to occupy her mind constantly. To get her mind off of it, she decides to go down the memory lane and remember what it was like to be a kid again. When she discovers her childhood doll house, a wave of nostalgia hits her and all she can wish for is to be small again, when nothing else mattered, except playing with her beloved childhood toy.

Feeling tired after a long journey home, she decides to take a nap. Once she awakens, her surroundings are are not the same, as she is clearly not in her bedroom, but nevertheless, there is something very familiar about the place she finds herself in. Eventually, the realization hits - her wish came true: She turned small, quite literally, and now has to wander around the rooms of her dollhouse.

With the help of her cat narrator, Nikki must explore each room, find hidden puzzle pieces that represent her childhood memories, and rebuild them on the puzzle board. Once all three rooms are complete, a magical key appears. Nikki picks it up and escapes the fever dream — waking up with a renewed appreciation for both her childhood and her future.

User Guideline

How to Run the Program

Installation

  • Unzip FeverDream_Binary.zip
  • Double click FeverDream.exe to start
  • No installation or additional software required
  • Windows 10/11 recommended

Controls

  • WASD - Move
  • Mouse - Look around
  • E - Pick up / Place objects / Remember
  • Space - Jump
  • ESC - Pause menu

How to Play

Start the game from the main menu. You can read the "How to Play" instructions there or jump right in by clicking Start. Nikki wakes up inside her dollhouse. Explore each room - the Playroom, Kitchen, and Bedroom - to find hidden puzzle pieces scattered around furniture and hard-to-reach places. Pick up a piece with E and carry it to the puzzle board. Place it in the matching slot to restore a piece of Nikki's memory. The cat narrator will give you hints after each placement.

Be careful - a wind-up toy soldier patrols each room. If it catches Nikki, she loses health. Your health persists across all rooms, so avoid the enemy whenever possible. Once all three rooms are complete, a golden key appears in the room you are in. Pick it up and Nikki escapes the fever dream.

You can pause the game at any time with ESC. From the pause menu you can continue, return to the main menu, or quit the game.

System Design

How the game is structured

Scenes - Three rooms, three memories

The dollhouse has 5 scenes - a main menu, three playable rooms, and an ending screen..

  • Playroom (Sofija)
  • Kitchen (Ilse)
  • Bedroom (Mahmut)
  • Ending - congratulations and chapter summary

The player can freely choose which room to enter next after completing a puzzle. Two doors in each room lead to the other two rooms.

Characters

Nikki —> main character. Player will be exploring the rooms throught the dollhouse where the goal is to find the right objects that will help Nikki return

Cat —> the narrator. Sits next to the puzzle board in each room. Gives hints about where to find puzzle pieces and tells stories about Nikki's childhood memories when she interacts with objects.

Wind-up toy —> Enemy. Patrols all rooms. When Nikki enters their detection range they start following her.

Audio

Background music — unique track per room, stops on scene change, restarts on entry.

Character sounds - jumping, walking, pushing, grabbing

Other characters - each character has sounds corresponding to their role.

Interaction sounds — puzzle piece placement, puzzle completion, object pickup, cat meow.

Player Systems

Nikki moves with WASD relative to the camera pivot direction. She can jump with physics-based impulse and BoxCast ground detection. Pressing E picks up the nearest interactable object using OverlapSphere with closest-object selection. Large furniture cannot be picked up but can be pushed by walking into it — physics-based force is applied on collision contact.

Puzzle System

Each room contains puzzle pieces hidden in hard-to-reach places. Every piece has a unique ID matched to a specific slot on the puzzle board. Nikki carries a piece to its correct slot and presses E to snap it in. The cat gives a hint about the next unfound piece after each placement. The last piece triggers no hint — instead the completion dialogue plays. When all slots are filled, the doors to other rooms unlock. If the player revisits a completed room, the puzzle auto-completes based on the GameManager's persisted state.

Enemy & Progression

The wind-up toy soldier uses a self-implemented procedural path generation system — no NavMesh or AI library. Perlin noise creates smooth organic wandering patterns. Three fan raycasts (left, center, right) detect obstacles and steering rules decide the avoidance direction. Distance-based detection switches between wander and chase states. A GameManager singleton with DontDestroyOnLoad persists health, room completion status, and key availability across all scenes. After all three rooms are complete, a golden key materialises. Nikki picks it up and the ending scene loads.

System Infrastructure

Class relationships

Player

  • PlayerMovement - WASD, camera-pivot-relative, Slerp rotation
  • PlayerJump - BoxCast ground check, LayerMask, impulse force
  • PlayerInteract - OverlapSphere, closest IInteractable, puzzle board detection
  • CameraPivotController - mouse yaw/pitch, clamped vertical
  • CameraFollow - dual SphereCast anti-clipping, distance recovery

Interaction

  • IInteractable - interface for all interactive objects
  • SmallProp - pickup with arc animation, carry, drop, cat dialogue
  • PuzzlePiece - pickup, carry, snap to board slot with ID matching
  • PushableObject - physics-based push on collision
  • StoryObject - inspect without pickup, triggers cat memory text
  • KeyMaterialise - appears after all rooms complete, triggers ending
  • InteractionPrompt - billboard UI above objects, distance-based

Systems

  • GameManager - singleton, DontDestroyOnLoad, persists health, room completion, key status
  • PuzzleBoard - ID matching, placement validation, completion trigger, auto-complete
  • PuzzleSlot - slotID, snap point transform, fill state
  • PuzzleCounter - live UI tracking of found pieces per room
  • DoorTrigger - scene transition on collision after puzzle completion
  • CatDialogue - queue-based dialogue with TypingEffect
  • CatNarrator - 3D cat, turns to player, speech bubble
  • LiquidHealthUI - custom HLSL shader heart with wave animation
  • ProceduralEnemyAI - Perlin noise wandering, fan raycast avoidance, chase FSM
Gameplay

How it plays

Nikki's Dollhouse is a navigation puzzle - the level design is the puzzle.Each room is a giant obstacle course where puzzle pieces are hidden in spaces that require exploration, climbing, and careful navigation to reach.

1

Wake up tiny

Nikki wakes up inside the dollhouse. The cat sits next to the puzzle board, ready to guide her.

2

Explore and jump

Each room is a giant obstacle course. Nikki can walk, jump, and push furniture. Puzzle pieces are hidden on top of furniture, behind objects, under cushions, and in tight spaces. Interactive objects with story text reveal Nikki's childhood memories.

3

Find and carry pieces

Press E to pick up a puzzle piece and carry it. Bring it back to the puzzle board on the floor. Pieces snap into place when dropped into the correct slot. The cat gives hints about where to find the next piece.

4

Avoid the enemy

A wind-up toy soldier patrols each room. It wanders and chases Nikki when she gets too close. Health persists across all rooms - be careful!

a key materialises. The cat nudges Nikki toward the front door.

5

Complete and move on

When all pieces are placed, the puzzle is complete and the doors to other rooms unlock. Choose which room to explore next. You can revisit completed rooms freely.

6

Find the key and escape

After all three rooms are complete, a golden key materialises. The cat tells Nikki it is time to go home. Pick up the key and escape the fever dream. The ending scene shows a congratulations message with each room as a chapter of Nikki's memories.
